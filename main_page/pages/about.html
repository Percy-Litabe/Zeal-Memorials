Who we are?

At ZEAL Tech, we specialize in creating thoughtful, personalized websites to honor and memorialize loved ones. Our platform is designed to help families share detailed funeral arrangements, celebrate cherished memories, and communicate vital information with ease and respect.

We understand that planning a funeral can be overwhelming, which is why we aim to ease the burden by providing a simple, streamlined way to create a digital space where loved ones can gather, share, and remember. From obituary details to service schedules, our websites offer a compassionate and practical solution for those facing a difficult time.

Our team is committed to delivering a seamless, user-friendly experience with a focus on care, respect, and privacy. Whether you are organizing a memorial, planning a service, or simply want to create a lasting tribute, we are here to help guide you every step of the way

What we offer.

We offer a range of compassionate services designed to support families through the funeral planning process. Our custom memorial websites allow you to create a personalized, user-friendly space to honor your loved one and share funeral details, including obituaries, service schedules, and locations.

You can also upload photos and videos to celebrate cherished memories, and create tribute videos or slideshows to further honor the deceased.

Our guestbook feature lets friends and family leave heartfelt messages. Additionally, families can update obituary information, and offer post-funeral communication,such as thank-you notes or photos. Our platform also includes grief support resources, ensuring that families have the tools they need during a difficult time.
