Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a funeral website?

A funeral website is an online space created to honor and remember a loved one. It can include obituary details, photos, funeral service information, and ways for friends and family to leave tributes.

2. How can a funeral website help my family or business?

It provides a central place to share information, memories, and updates, making it easier for family, friends, and the community to participate and stay informed.

3. Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we create personalized funeral websites that reflect the unique life and spirit of your loved one.

4. How long does it take to create a website?

Typically, a funeral website can be designed and launched within a few days, depending on your specific needs.

5. Can the website be updated after it's live?

Yes, you can easily update the website with new information, photos, or messages as needed.

6. Can I add a donation link to the website?

Absolutely. We can integrate donation links for charities or memorial funds directly into the website.

7. Is the website mobile-friendly?

Yes, all of our funeral websites are fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly on both computers and mobile devices.

8. Can visitors leave messages or condolences on the site?

Yes, we can include a guestbook or tribute section where visitors can share their thoughts, memories, and condolences.

9. Do I need any technical skills to manage the website?

No technical skills are required. We provide easy-to-follow instructions for you to manage the website, and we're always available to assist if needed.

10. Is there a cost for the website?

Yes, there is a fee for our services, but we offer different pricing packages based on your needs and the features you choose.
